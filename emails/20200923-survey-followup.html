Hello Friends of BarCamp Philly,

Thank you to all who shared thoughts in our 2020 BarCamp Philly survey. We learned a lot, but overwhelmingly, we heard one thing:

It sounds like you all want to have a BarCamp this year.

We have worked on making some initial plans, that include how we'll be meeting (it will be accessible from your favorite desktop, laptop or mobile device) and when we'll be meeting (it will be in 2020, and on a Saturday.)

Look out for our next email where you can weigh in on session topics!

The next thing we will be doing is sending out a survey to gather ideas for sessions – that you'd want to host yourself and/or that you'd love to attend.

Since a primary opportunity of having a fully virtual event is that we bring BarCamp to YOU, it means you can bring whatever you've got going on to BarCamp. Some early ideas:
  • Cooking demos
  • Guided walks through a park or trail near you
  • Group share of work from home setups
  • Houseplant tutorials
There will, of course, still be ample room to share your latest CMS learnings, UX research techniques, community organizing tactics, startup stories, and historical fiction writing sessions. Anything and everything that was shared at past BarCamps is absolutely still a welcome topic to continue to explore. But we think this year is a great opportunity to expand.

You can also tweet your session ideas or big dreams to us at @BarCampPhilly. Or find us on Facebook and Instagram.

Thank you for being a friend.

Until next time,
The BarCamp Philly Team
