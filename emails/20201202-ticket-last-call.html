Hello BarCampers of Philly,

It's that time of year again–BarCamp week. And though it looks a little different than in past years, we are excited to be bringing you an awesome 30 talk lineup this year, that will be all-virtual.

Ready for BarCamp Philly?

We are doing all of our chatting & connecting in a virtual version of the Forum, on Discord. If you've already snagged your ticket to BarCamp, you'll be getting an invite to the Discord server this week. And if you still need a ticket, go ahead and get one this week and we will ensure you are onto the server before the big day.

Get Your Ticket Today

More details on the website

We have added some details around the day to the BarCamp Philly 2020 website. You'll see the basics of the schedule, get tips on what to do to prepare, and even get a little more information on what virtual BarCamp will be compared to an in-person event.

We have an amazing group of volunteers already onboarded, but could definitely make room for a few more, especially when it comes to moderating talks. Sign up here to get trained on how to moderate a talk or channel at BarCamp.

Sign Up to Volunteer

Join us at 5:00pm for the BarCamp Philly after party

We are excited to be hosting PhillyAV at this year's after party. PhillyAV is a collective of artists working across digital media, who will be sharing live and recorded performances over Zoom starting at 5:00pm. Check out their work and follow them on Instagram @phillyav.

Thanks for joining us on this year's adventure.

Big applause to our friends at Wharton, for bringing this virtual event to fruition with your partnership, to all of our volunteers who are helping us build the event, to everyone who is giving a talk and bringing the content to the unconference, and to all of our attendees who are sticking with us.

As always, you can tweet your session ideas or big dreams to us at @BarCampPhilly. Or find us on Facebook and Instagram.

May your week bring a few moments of unexpected joy.

Until next time,
The BarCamp Philly Team
